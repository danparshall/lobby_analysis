. Details of weighted indicators

*The CPI and Roth weights were normalised to the Bednarova framework
(original weights shown in parenthesis). The weights given for each
framework were summed (total column). Scores 10 and higher were weighted
3, scores 1 and higher were weighted 2. We also identified four
indicators that we believe are crucial to transparency and also warrant
a weight of 3 based on our experience analysing lobbyist registers.
These are highlighted in yellow.

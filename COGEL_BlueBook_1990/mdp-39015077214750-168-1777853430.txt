LOBBYISTS: REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
(As of January 1, 1990)
(c) Limited to lobbyists before agencies in the executive branch.
(d) All lobbyists are registered regardless of whether they have expenses or not.
(e) Anyone who spends or receives $500 or more communicating with legislative officials.
(f) All who provide anything of value to legislators.
(g) Gifts to elected officials.
(h) Contingent fee agreements; individual "gifts" if over $15 a day.
(i) Gifts, honoraria or payments made to any state officer or employee during a month that exceeds an aggregate amount or value of $20 per officer or employee must show the
name and addres of each officer or employee, a description or statement of purpose and the amount.
(j) Person or firm he represents.
(k) l.e., food and beverage, mass mailings and other lobbying expenditures.
(1) Names of persons compensated more than $16.00 during any 12 month period for lobbying.
(m) Those whose activities require registration with the board.
(n) Must furnish to the lobbyist five days before a reporting date information on employers' lobbying disbursements.
(0) Individuals employed as lobbyists.
(p) Monthly if $5,000+ spent in any month.
(q) An interim report is also required.
(r) If over $250.
(s) Monthly reporting only during legislative sessions. Report for period between sessions.
(t) Reporting at the end of a legislative session.
(u) Listing of legislative bills and resolutions and positions taken.
(v) (This) applies of expenditures of lobby registrant exceeds $50 during previous month.
(w) All.
(x) Only expenditures made on behalf of legislators, the Governor or his staff when specific legislation is discussed are required to be reported.
(y) The amount of compensation does not have to be reported if the lobbyist is a full-time salaried employee or on retainer for their client.
(z) Volunteer lobbyists is not a full-time employee for employer nor on annual retainer.
(aa) Only is lobbyist is not a full-time employee for employer nor on annual retainer.
(bb) Over $25 spent on one legislator or public official per year.
(cc) Expenditures over $37.50 on single occasion in aggregate of $300 in 12 month period.
(dd) Those having contact with state officials and spending over a threshold amount of time.
(ee) Even numbered years: 01/31, 07/31; Odd numbered years: 01/31, 04/30, 07/31.
(ff) Employers report total, which includes compensation.
(gg) Only if over $50 per week.
(hh) Contributions over $100.
(ii) All persons required to be registered under the Act.
(jj) Must provide list of specific categories of subject matters and the designation assigned to that legislation or action, if known.
(kk) March 10, covering January and February. January 20, covering entire preceding calendar year.
(II) Names of entities paying over $500 to association or organization for which lobbying is done.
(mm) Contemporaneous record of lobbying activities; identification of rules and bills on which lobbying occurs.
(nn) No reports are required, other than registration. See W.S. 28-7-101.

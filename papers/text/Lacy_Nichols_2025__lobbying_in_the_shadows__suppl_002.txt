Australia

Neither PMI nor BAT were listed when we searched for their names (and
aliases) in the main search function. Based on a previous study we had
conducted in which we extracted, cleaned and linked data from the
federal, state and territory lobbyist registers in Australia, we
identified three lobby firms that had previously represented PMI and
five for BAT in at least one Australian jurisdiction. Based on this, we
did a targeted search of the client histories for each firm, identifying
six firms that had previously represented a tobacco company. In the
absence of this data, it would be too time-consuming to manually search
the history of the 337 organisations currently registered.

Canada

When Philip Morris was searched, it came up with 155 registrations for:
Philip Morris companies (1996-2003), Medicago (2020-2021, a vaccine
manufacturer)[1], Kraft Canada (1996-2005), and Rothmans, Benson &
Hedges Inc. (1999-2024, a subsidiary of Philip Morris International
Inc.).

When British American tobacco was searched, it came up with 82
registrations for: Imperial Tobacco Canada Limited (2005-2024, parent
company British American Tobacco); Nicoventures Ltd. (2015-2016, parent
company British American Tobacco)

All ‘active’ returns are documented below. Meeting counts are only for
currently active in-house and consulting firms, further analysis of
inactive consultant lobbyists would reveal more meetings.
